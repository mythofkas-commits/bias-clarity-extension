Privacy & Security

  • API keys are never stored in the extension
  • Text content is sent to the configured backend only
  • The extension does not track or store your browsing history
  • Local analysis mode processes text entirely in your browser

About

Argument Clarifier helps you understand the structure of arguments without making truth judgments. It identifies claims, reasoning patterns, language cues, and potential logical issues.
